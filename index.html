About Us

Connecting Souls Through Prayer and Fellowship

Our mission is to unite believers from all walks of life in a spiritual journey of prayer, fasting, and community. We believe in the power of prayer to transform lives and bring healing, hope, and restoration.

Founded By You & I

Through our online platform, we connect individuals with dedicated prayer partners, organize powerful night prayer sessions, and bring believers together for retreats, fasting, and deep spiritual conversations. Join us as we build a strong, Christ-centered community committed to making a difference through faith and prayer.

Our Foundation

Our Guiding Constitution

3 AM YADAH ENCOUNTER is an ecumenical youth organization founded on 5th June 2024 by Brian Alubale. Our constitution provides the framework for our operations, membership, and leadership as we work to spread the Gospel and serve our community.

Spiritual Foundation

Spreading the Gospel and winning souls to Christ

Youth Empowerment

Nurturing talents and providing mentorship

Community Service
